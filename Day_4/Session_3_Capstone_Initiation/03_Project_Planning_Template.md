# Project Planning Template

> **Goal:** Before you write code, you must plan. Successful projects are "Designed" before they are "Built."

---

##  Simple Project Milestone Tracking

| Milestone | Deliverable | Deadline | Owner |
|-----------|-------------|----------|-------|
| **M1: Definition** | Final Problem Statement & KPIs defined. | Day 4 EOD | PM |
| **M2: Cleaning** | Python script cleans the raw CSV into a clean SQL format. | Day 5 Morning | Eng |
| **M3: Modeling** | Star Schema built in BI Tool. | Day 5 Early Afternoon | Viz |
| **M4: Storytelling** | Presentation slides & Dashboard finalized. | Day 5 3:00 PM | BA |

---

##  Dashboard Wireframing (The "How")

Draw your dashboard design on paper before opening Power BI/Tableau.
- Where will the KPI cards go?
- What chart will you use for the 5-year trend? (Line or Bar?)
- Which slicers are absolutely necessary?

---

##  The Business Pitch Strategy

Structure your final presentation around these four slides:
1. **The Hook**: The #1 problem we discovered.
2. **The Evidence**: 2-3 deep-dive charts showing the root cause.
3. **The Solution**: Your proposed business decision.
4. **The Value**: How much money/time will this decision save?

---

##  Group Discussion
- What is our biggest "Risk" (e.g., data quality issues)?
- How will we resolve disagreements in the team?
- Who will be the first person to speak during the presentation?

# Capstone Project: Overview & Guidelines

> **The Final Challenge:** Combine everything you have learned  Python, Pandas, SQL, Visualization, and BI Dashboards  to solve a real-world business problem from start to finish.

---

##  What is the Capstone?

The Capstone Project is your chance to show you are "Job-Ready." You will work in a small group to analyze a complex dataset and present your findings to the class.

---

##  Project Requirements

Your project must include the following 4 phases:

1. **Phase 1: Data Strategy**: Define your Problem Statement and KPIs.
2. **Phase 2: Data Engineering**: Use **Python/Pandas** to clean the data and **SQL** to query it.
3. **Phase 3: Visual Analytics**: Build an interactive **BI Dashboard** (Power BI/Tableau) showing your key insights.
4. **Phase 4: The Pitch**: Present a 10-minute "Insight-to-Decision" presentation using the **Minto Pyramid** structure.

---

##  Group Formation

- **3-4 Members per group**.
- **Roles recommended**: 
    - 1 Data Engineer (SQL/Python expert).
    - 1 Visualization Specialist (Dashboard expert).
    - 1 Business Analyst (Insight/Presentation expert).
    - 1 Project Manager (Planning/Gantt chart expert).

---

##  The 2-Day Timeline

- **Day 4 Session 3**: Team formation and Problem Statement selection.
- **Day 5 (Full Day)**: Dedicated project work, debugging, and final presentations.

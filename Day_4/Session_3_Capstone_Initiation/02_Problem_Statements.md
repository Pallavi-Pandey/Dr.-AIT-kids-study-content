# Capstone Problem Statements

> **Instruction:** Each team must select ONE of the following scenarios. You will be provided with a raw dataset (CSV/SQL) matching your selection.

---

##  Option 1: E-Commerce Retention Deep-Dive
- **Scenario**: A major online fashion brand has high website traffic but their "Repeat Purchase Rate" is dropping.
- **Goal**: Identify why customers aren't returning and which categories (Shoes vs. Apparel) are suffering the most.
- **KPIs**: Churn Rate, AOV, Customer Lifetime Value (CLV).

##  Option 2: Healthcare Operational Efficiency
- **Scenario**: A private hospital wants to reduce patient wait times without hiring more doctors.
- **Goal**: Analyze surgery schedules and emergency room data to find "Bottlenecks" (slow spots).
- **KPIs**: Average Wait Time, Staff Utilization Rate, Patient Satisfaction Score.

##  Option 3: Fintech Fraud Detection
- **Scenario**: A digital bank noticed a 10% increase in "Suspicious Transactions" in Tier-2 cities.
- **Goal**: Use Data Analysis to spot patterns in fraudulent behavior (e.g., time of day, amount size).
- **KPIs**: Fraud Loss Ratio, Detection Accuracy, False-Positive Rate.

##  Option 4: Supply Chain & Inventory Optimization
- **Scenario**: A retail chain is losing Rs. 50 Lakhs a month because product 'Milk' expires before it's sold.
- **Goal**: Balance "Stock-on-hand" vs. "Consumer Demand" to reduce waste.
- **KPIs**: Inventory Turnover, Stock-out Rate, Waste Percentage.

---

##  Discovery Questions for Your Team
- What is the single most important metric for your CEO?
- Does your dataset have enough columns to calculate that metric?
- Who is your "Target Audience" for the final dashboard?

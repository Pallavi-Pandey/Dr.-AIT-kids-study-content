# Customer Insights & Segmentation

> **Learning Goal:** Learn how to group customers into segments to provide personalized experiences and maximize loyalty.

---

##  What is Segmentation?

Not all customers are equal. Treating a "Once-a-year" shopper the same as a "Weekly VIP" is a mistake. **Segmentation** is the art of dividing your crowd into meaningful groups.

---

## 1. Demographic vs. Behavioral

| Type | What it is | Example |
|------|-----------|---------|
| **Demographic** | Who they are | Age, Gender, City, Income |
| **Behavioral** | What they DO | Frequency of purchase, Preferred payment, Average spend |

**Why Behavioral is King**: A 25-year-old and a 60-year-old might both buy the same expensive Camera. Their *behavior* (Photography lovers) is more important than their *age*.

---

##  The RFM Model (Professional Gold Standard)

**What**: A scoring system to rank customers.
- **Recency (R)**: How many days since their last purchase? (Lower is better).
- **Frequency (F)**: How many times have they bought in total? (Higher is better).
- **Monetary (M)**: How much total money have they spent? (Higher is better).

---

##  How to Use RFM Scores

| Segment | RFM Score | Description | Action |
|---------|-----------|-------------|--------|
| **Champions** | 5, 5, 5 | Spent a lot, buy often, recently visited. | Reward them with early access to new products. |
| **At Risk** | 1, 4, 4 | Used to buy often, but haven't visited in months. | Send a "We Miss You" 20% discount coupon. |
| **Newbies** | 5, 1, 1 | Just made their first purchase. | Send a "Welcome" guide or a second-purchase offer. |

---

##  Industry Tip: The "Pareto Principle" in Retail

In most businesses, **80% of revenue comes from 20% of customers.** 
- **Where**: Use segmentation to find that 20% and protect them at all costs!

##  Quick Check Questions

1. Which is more useful for a sports brand: knowing a customer's age or knowing they bought running shoes 3 times this year?
2. If a customer has a High Monetary score but a Low Recency score, what segment do they belong to?
3. How can segmenting by `payment_method` help a store optimize its checkout counter?
